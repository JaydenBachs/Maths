About Us

ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

Chrisland University Mathematics Department is a scholarly unit focused on advancing mathematical knowledge. It comprises faculty members specializing in pure and applied mathematics, offering undergraduate and graduate programs. The department supports research through specialized centers, provides resources like libraries and labs, and encourages student engagement through clubs and events. Collaborations with other departments and external organizations contribute to interdisciplinary research, while seminars and conferences facilitate the exchange of ideas among faculty and students. Overall, the mathematics department plays a vital role in education, research, and the promotion of mathematical understanding within the university.

MISSION STATEMENT

Cultivate students' capacity to apply scientific knowledge to real-world challenges, impart versatile skills applicable in scientific fields, and furnish a foundation for advanced studies in diverse scientific disciplines.

VISION STATEMENT

The university hopes to attract talented youths, to teach and inspire them to advance the realm of knowledge by cultivating their faculty of academic enquiry, creative scholarships, research discovery and the application of new knowledge to the transformation of the individual and society.
